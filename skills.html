Skills/Goals

As I said in my "About me" section, I aim to achieve a career in the tech industry through
whatever form it may be; whether it's as a software, app, or game developer. The reason I'm
so keen on earning a position such as this is simply due to the fact that I find technology
and anything computers to be fun, challenging, and the key to leading humanity to new and,
previously thought to be impossible, heights. I also make sure to maintain a growth mindset
when creating aspirations for myself. As long as I believe that I can do something and keep
putting in work to achieve that something, I'm bound to succeed eventually.

I applied this mindset while my two teamates and I were developing a game together for the second
week milestone project of the All Star Code Summer Intensive. We were given just a weekend to
finish the entire thing, and I had tasked myself with writing most of the script. It was quite
challenging and even tiring at points, but I didn't stop working on my part until I had created
something which my teamates and I could be proud of. The main reason that I took most of the
developement as my role was for me to personally challenge myself and see what I could accomplish
under a strict time-frame. The image you see on the right is a snippet of the code I wrote for game,
and the game itself can be played on my GitHub page. There, you can also see various other projects
that I have created, either as part of All Star Code or something I did during my free time.
